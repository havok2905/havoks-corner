Lightning Rifle

Weapon (firearm), legendary

Ranged Weapon, Ammunition ( Arcane Battery ), Range ( 60/140 ), Reload ( 4 shots ), Two Handed. 3d8+DEX Lightning Damage

Misfire: When a natural 1 is rolled on an attack roll, make a DC 13 DEX saving throw. On a failure, you take the damage you would have dealt. On a success, you take half as much.
