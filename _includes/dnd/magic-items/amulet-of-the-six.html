Amulet of the Six

Wondrous item, varying rarity (requires attunement for 8 hours)

This amulet bears the symbol of a deity inlaid with precious stones or metals. Each deity grants the casting of a corresponding spell, listed below, based on rarity. The amulet has two charges and recharges in 24 hours. Wisdom is your spellcasting modifier for these spells. Your AC increases by 1 while wearing this amulet.

Symbol

  • Void: Black Circle
  • Light: White Circle
  • Presence: A Face
  • Movement: An Arrow
  • Force: Sunburst
  • Will: Flame

Common

  • Void: Sleep
  • Light: Color Spray
  • Presence: Unseen Servant
  • Movement: Expeditious Retreat
  • Force: Shield
  • Will: Heroism

Rare

  • Void: Invisibility
  • Light: Branding Smite
  • Presence: Alter Self
  • Movement: Misty Step
  • Force: Shatter
  • Will: Detect Thoughts

Legendary

  • Void: Banishing Smite
  • Light: Dawn
  • Presence: Conjure Elemental
  • Movement: Animate Objects
  • Force: Wall of Force
  • Will: Telekinesis
