Raven's Rapier

Weapon (rapier), artifact (requires attunement)

Melee Weapon (martial, sword), Finesse. 1d8+DEX magical piercing damage.

As a bonus action, if the attack roll for an attack with this weapon succeeds, you may cast the spell Misty Step without any verbal, material, or somatic components, without the use of a spell slot or the spellcasting feature.
