Shock Gloves

Melee Weapon, very rare

Unarmed: This item replaces your unarmed attack.

Glove: This item cannot be disarmed against the player's will. You can use other items while holding this glove, but can only make attacks with the glove while the hand is unoccupied.

Charge: This item must be recharged after 1 minute of being activated. Charging takes one hour.
