Cartridge Blade

Weapon, very rare

Any melee weapon may be outfitted with a modification that allows arcane cartridges to power it's attacks. These cartridges vary in intensity and ability, and may only be loaded one at a time.

As a bonus action on your turn, you may load your cartridge blade with one cartridge. It can then expended as a reaction, causing the effects to the cartridge to take place.

Modification

Modification of a cartridge weapon is expensive and finding those skilled in the craft can be quite difficult. It is not uncommon for the cost of modification to exceed several hundred gold pieces.

Common Cartridges

  • Elemental Cartridge: Deals 1d10 extra elemental damage. Each cartridge may only have one designated element.
  • Absorb Elements Cartridge: Casts Absorb Elements
