Actions

Multiattack. The Commanding Sin Eater makes two attacks, only one of which can be a Radiant Impale.

Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5ft., one creature. Hit: 1d8+4 slashing damage and 2d6 radiant damage.

Radiant Impale (Recharge 5=6). Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., Hit: 2d8+4 piercing damage plus 2d6 radiant damage. The target's hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the radiant damage taken, and the sin eater regains hit points equal to that amount. The reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target must succeed a DC 15 CHA saving throw. On a failure, the target will transform, slowly, into a sin eater over the course of seven days. If a target would reduce to zero hit points from this attack, they reduce to one hit point instead.
