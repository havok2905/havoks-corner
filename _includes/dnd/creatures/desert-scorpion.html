Actions

Multiattack. The Desert Scorpion makes two claw attacks and one stinger attack.

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: (3d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage. The target is grappled (Escape DC 18) The scorpion has two claws, each of which can grapple only one target.

Sting. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature. Hit: (3d10 + 5) piercing damage. The target must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw, taking 6d10 poison damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful save.

Legendary Actions

The Desert Scorpion can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Desert Scorpion regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Detect. The Desert Scorpion makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.

Burrow ( Costs 3 Actions ). The Desert Scorpion makes a dash action, burrowing 30ft vertically under the sand and 30ft in a random direction. It surfaces at the start of its next turn. Any creature within 10ft. of the Desert Scorpion must succeed a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw or take 4d10 bludgeoning damage.

Claw ( Costs 2 Actions ). The Desert Scorpion makes one claw attack.
