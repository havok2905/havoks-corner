Regeneration. The doctor regains 10 hit points at the start of each of its turns if it has at least 1 hit point and isn't in sun-light. If the doctor takes radiant damage, this trait doesn't function until the start of the doctor's next turn.

Spider Climb. The doctor can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

Sunlight Hypersensitivity. The doctor takes 20 radiant damage when it starts its turn in sunlight. While in sunlight, it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.

Unusual Nature. The doctor does not require air.

Spellcasting. This creature is an 4th-level spellcaster. Their spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 15; +7 to hit with spell attacks). They regain their expended spell slots when they finishes a short or long rest. They know the following warlock spells.

  • Cantrips (at will): infestation, mage hand, message, minor illusion, thaumaturgy, thorn whip
  • 1st-2nd Level (2 2nd level slots): bane, cause fear, hellish rebuke, blindness/deafness, phantasmal force, false life+, disguise self

Actions

Multiattack. The doctor makes two Claw attacks followed by one Bite attack. If both Claw attacks hit the same creature, the Bite attack is made with advantage.

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d8 + 5) slashing damage.

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. If the target is missing any of its hit points, it instead takes 11 (2d10) necrotic damage.

The target's hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the necrotic damage taken, and the doctor regains hit points equal to that amount. The reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if its hit point maximum is reduced to 0.

Blood Disgorge (Recharge 5-6). The doctor vomits blood in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 18 (4d8) necrotic damage, and it can't regain hit points for 1 minute. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage with no additional effects.
